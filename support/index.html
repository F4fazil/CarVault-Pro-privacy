Contact Support

You can reach CarVault Pro support by email at qazifazil59@gmail.com.

Please include the email address on your account, your device type, your app version if available, and a short description of what you need help with.

Report Problems

To report a problem, send a clear description of the issue and the steps that caused it. Screenshots, error messages, and details about recent app actions can help us understand and fix the issue.

You can also use the support email for feedback, missing features, subscription questions, account access issues, or privacy and data requests related to CarVault Pro.

Response Time

We aim to review support messages within 2 business days. Some requests may take longer if we need additional details or need to investigate a technical issue.

What should I send when reporting a bug?

Include your device model, operating system version, CarVault Pro app version, the feature you were using, and the steps to reproduce the problem.
